Warehouse LED Lighting Layout Guide for Importers and Contractors

A practical overview of warehouse lighting decisions, fixture selection and quote preparation.

warehouse lighting solution

Collect site data first

Mounting height, rack layout, aisle width and working hours are needed before recommending wattage and beam angle.

Choose fixtures by zone

Use linear fixtures for low and medium-height zones, high bays for tall areas and tri-proof lights for humid or dusty spaces.

Prepare the RFQ

Include drawings, target lux, voltage, certification needs, country, quantity and installation deadline.
