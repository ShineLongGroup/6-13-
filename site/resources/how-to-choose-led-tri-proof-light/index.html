How to Choose LED Tri-proof Lights for Parking Garages

A buyer-focused guide to IP rating, wiring, optics, power range and supplier checks for LED tri-proof lights.

how to choose LED tri proof light

Start with the environment

Check humidity, dust, ceiling height, wiring route and whether the garage needs linkable fixtures or rear connection.

Match IP and material requirements

IP65 is suitable for many indoor garages, while IP66 or IP69K options are useful when cleaning pressure, corrosion or agricultural environments are involved.

Ask for project documents

Request spec sheets, certification files, packing details, installation guidance and replacement policy before placing bulk orders.
